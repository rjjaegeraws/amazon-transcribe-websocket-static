Using the Amazon Transcribe Medical WebSocket API

This demo uses Amazon Cognito to authenticate and authorize users to interatct with the Amazon Transcribe Medical API. To begin, click the 'SignIn' button below and either register an account or login. You will be redirected back to this page and the AWS credentials will populate if successful.

Click the 'Start' button to begin. Your browser may prompt you for access to the microphone; grant the permissions and continue. Once recording has started, the application is streaming your speech to be converted to accurate text in real-time.
